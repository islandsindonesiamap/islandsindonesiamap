  • Palm Coast: A rapidly growing city located on the northern edge of the county, Palm Coast is a mix of residential neighborhoods, commercial centers, and natural preserves.

  • Hastings: A historic agricultural community, Hastings is known for its citrus groves, timberlands, and vibrant rural lifestyle.
